Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 9, 2026

Artelios ("we," "us," or "our") operates a web application that helps Etsy sellers manage their shop listings. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly and information we receive through the Etsy API when you authorize our application:

  • Etsy shop data: Shop ID, listing details (titles, descriptions, images, prices, quantities), and shop settings — accessed only after you grant permission via Etsy's OAuth 2.0 flow.
  • Usage data: How you interact with our application (pages visited, features used) to help us improve the service.
  • Contact information: Email address if you contact us or sign up for our waitlist.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create, update, and manage Etsy listings on your behalf via the official Etsy API.
  • To generate AI-assisted product images and descriptions for your review and approval.
  • To provide analytics on your shop's listing performance.
  • To send you service-related communications (you may opt out at any time).
  • To improve and develop our application.

3. Etsy API Data

Artelios uses the Etsy API to read and write listing data on your behalf. We request only the OAuth scopes necessary for the features you use. We do not sell, rent, or share your Etsy shop data with third parties, except as required to operate the service (e.g., AI image generation APIs).

Your Etsy access token is stored securely and used only to perform actions you've authorized. You can revoke access at any time from your Etsy account settings under Apps & Connections.

4. AI-Generated Content

When you use our AI image or description generation features, your product details may be sent to third-party AI providers to generate content. We disclose AI-generated content in every Etsy listing we create on your behalf, in compliance with Etsy's seller policies.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the service. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at hello@artelios.art.

6. Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including encrypted storage of credentials and HTTPS for all data transmission.

7. Third-Party Services

Our service integrates with:

  • Etsy API — to manage your shop listings.
  • AI image generation APIs — to generate product visuals.

Each third-party service has its own privacy policy governing how they handle data.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. To exercise these rights, contact us at hello@artelios.art.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We'll notify you of significant changes by posting a notice on our website or sending an email.

10. Contact

Questions about this policy? Contact us at hello@artelios.art.
