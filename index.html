How Artelios Works

A simple, transparent workflow that respects your shop and Etsy's platform rules.

1

Connect your Etsy shop

Authorize Artelios via the official Etsy OAuth flow. We request only the permissions needed (listings read/write). No password sharing.

2

Set up your product catalog

Upload your product details or import from your existing Etsy shop. Artelios generates titles, descriptions, and images using AI.

3

Publish and manage listings

Artelios creates listings on Etsy via the official API. Every listing clearly discloses AI-generated content per Etsy's seller policies.

4

Track performance

Monitor views, favorites, and sales. Artelios surfaces actionable insights to help you improve your shop over time.
